**Profile Setup:**
- Professional headshot
- Headline: "Building Modern Websites for Clinics & SaaS 🚀"

**Outreach Script (Personalized):**

```

I noticed your [clinic/startup/business] on LinkedIn. I've been designing modern websites for businesses like yours, and I wanted to share something:

[SPECIFIC OBSERVATION]: "Your clinic has great reviews, but your website doesn't match that quality."

[Your Name]

**Conversion Rate:** 20-30% of responses → actual projects

## PHASE 4: CLOSING YOUR FIRST CLIENTS

### 4.1 Free Audit/Consultation Call

**Your Offer:**
"Free website audit - 20 minutes"
- Analyze their current site
- Identify 3 quick wins
- Show them what competitors are doing
- Recommend a package if relevant
- No pressure, no upsell

**Why this works:**
- Low barrier to saying yes
- You get to talk to them directly
- 30-40% of audits lead to actual projects
- Builds trust and credibility

### 4.2 Discovery Call to Project

**Questions to ask (15-20 min):**
1. "What's your main goal with a new/improved website?"
2. "How many patients/customers are you getting now?"
3. "What's your biggest frustration with your current site?" (if exists)
4. "What's your timeline?"
5. "What's your approximate budget?"

**Don't pitch yet.** Just listen.

### 4.3 Send Proposal (within 24 hours)

**One-page PDF with:**
- Their business name at the top
- Specific recommendation (which tier: Starter/Pro/Premium)
- Why this tier (connect to their goals)
- Timeline
- What's included
- Pricing
- Next steps
- Your signature

### 4.4 Follow-up Sequence (if no response)

- Day 1: Send proposal
- Day 3: "Just checking if you got the proposal. Happy to answer questions."
- Day 5: "Would love to move forward. When are you free to start?"
- Day 7: "If timing isn't right now, let's connect in Q2. I'm saving a spot for you."
