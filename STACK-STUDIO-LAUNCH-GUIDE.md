## STEP 6: BOOK CONSULTATION CALLS (Week 3-4)

### When They Respond

**Their message:** "Sure, tell me more"
**Your response (within 2 hours):**
```
Great! Here's what I'm thinking:

I'd love to spend 20 minutes looking at your website and your business goals.

I'll give you:
1. Specific areas where you're losing patients/customers
2. 3 quick wins to implement
3. A rough estimate of what a modern website would cost

No pressure, just valuable insight.

Are you free for a quick call this week? [Calendar link]

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Consultation Call (20 min)

**Script:**
1. **Warm-up (2 min):** "Thanks for taking the time. How's your [clinic/business] doing?"
2. **Listen (5 min):** "Tell me about your main challenges with your current website"
3. **Observe (5 min):** "Let me show you what competitors are doing" (share screen)
4. **Offer (5 min):** "I think package X would be perfect for you because..."
5. **Close (3 min):** "Would you like to move forward?"

### If They Say Yes
- Send proposal within 24 hours
- Ask for 50% deposit to secure spot
- Schedule kickoff meeting

### If They Say "Let Me Think"
- "No problem. When should I check back in?" (set specific date)
- Follow up on that date via email/WhatsApp

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### ✅ DO THIS:
- Be genuinely helpful (no hard sell)
- Respond fast (within 2 hours)
- Follow up persistently (3-5x before giving up)
- Show results (use metrics and case studies)
- Be specific (mention their clinic/business by name)
- Provide free value first (audit, tips, advice)

### ❌ DON'T DO THIS:
- Send generic messages ("Looking for web design services?")
- Undersell (don't accept ₹20k for a ₹75k project)
- Disappear (respond to every lead consistently)
- Overpromise (be realistic about timelines)
- Spam groups (share value, not sales pitches)
- Give up (persistence is key)
