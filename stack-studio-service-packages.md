# stack.studio - Service Packages & Pricing Strategy

## THREE-TIER SERVICE MODEL

Your agency should offer three clear packages based on client budgets and needs:

---

## TIER 1: STARTER 🚀
**Perfect for: Startups, small businesses, side projects**

### Package: Essential Website
- **Price: ₹30,000 - ₹50,000** (or $360-600 USD)
- **Timeline: 2-3 weeks**
- **Deliverables:**
  - Single-page or 3-5 page website
  - Mobile responsive design
  - Basic SEO optimization
  - Contact form
  - 3 rounds of revisions
  - 30 days free hosting/support

### Tech Stack:
- HTML + Tailwind CSS (fastest to deliver)
- OR Astro (fastest loading)
- SEO meta tags
- Google Analytics setup

### Target Clients:
- Freelancers launching online
- Small local services (plumbers, consultants)
- Early-stage startups
- Content creators / bloggers

### Sales Pitch:
> "Get your business online with a fast, professional website. No code complexity—pure modern design and great performance. Built in 2-3 weeks."

---

## TIER 2: PROFESSIONAL 💼
**Perfect for: Growing businesses, professionals, clinics**

- **Deliverables:**

  - Appointment/booking system (for clinics)

  - 90 days free support

### Tech Stack:
- React or Next.js
- Tailwind CSS

- Email service (Mailchimp, Brevo)
- Optional: Headless CMS

### Target Clients:
- Established small businesses
- Professional clinics & healthcare
- Agencies & design studios
- Consultants & coaches
- Local businesses wanting online presence

### Key Differentiators:
- Conversion-optimized layouts
- Booking system integration (for clinics)

> "A professional website that works. Appointment bookings, email capture, beautiful animations—everything designed to convert visitors into customers. 90+ Lighthouse scores, under 3s load time."

3. "Modern tech stack means your site stays relevant for 5+ years."

### Why Clients Say YES:
- **Transparency:** Clear packages, no hidden costs
- **Results:** Proof of metrics (speed, Lighthouse, conversions)
- **Speed:** Quick delivery (2-3 weeks for Starter)
- **Ongoing:** Options for support and optimization
- **Expertise:** You mention specific tech (Next.js, React, Astro)

---

## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

### Price Anchoring:
Show all three tiers. Most clients pick the middle (Professional) tier.

**Starter → Professional → Premium**
- Make Professional look like the "sweet spot"
- Make Premium feel premium (long-term partner)
- Make Starter feel like good value (quick win)

### Value Stacking:
Don't just say "₹75,000"
Instead:
> "Professional Package: ₹75,000
> - 5-10 custom pages ($3k value)
> - Booking system integration ($2k value)
> - Email automation setup ($1.5k value)
> - 90+ Lighthouse performance ($2k value)
> - 90 days free support ($1.5k value)
> - **Total value: ₹1,50,000+ for ₹75,000**"

### Payment Terms:
- **50% upfront** to secure your spot
- **50% on delivery** before going live
- OR **3 installments:** 40% start, 30% midway, 30% delivery

## WHAT NOT TO DO

❌ Charge by hourly rate (unsustainable, unclear value)
❌ Offer unlimited revisions (cap at 3-10 rounds)
❌ Build without a proposal (always written agreement)
❌ Include unlimited maintenance (cap it or charge monthly)
❌ Promise specific SEO rankings (you can't control everything)
❌ Undervalue your work to seem "budget-friendly"
❌ Quote without understanding scope first
